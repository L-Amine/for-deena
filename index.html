A Promise From Amine

For Deena

Not because you asked for it, but because my heart wanted to say it.

I

I promise to be patient

I know feelings can be scary sometimes. I will never rush you or force you into anything. Your comfort and happiness will always matter more than my impatience.

II

I promise to be honest

No games, no pretending, no hidden intentions. What I feel for you is real, and I will always be sincere with you.

III

I promise to support you

In your good days and difficult days, I want to be someone you can rely on, trust, and smile with.

IV

I promise to respect your heart

Your feelings are precious. I will never take them for granted or treat them carelessly.

V

I promise my feelings are genuine

Whether it takes time or not, what I feel for you comes from a place of honesty, admiration, and care.

“I'm not asking you to decide today.
I'm only asking you to know that my feelings are real.”

Amine ♥
